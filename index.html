About Khetipati

Khetipati Organics Pvt. Ltd. is a newly established company with its base in Kathmandu and functionality in Dhankuta Municipality. With a focus on large scale commercial farming and agro-processing, Khetipati Organics will promote locally produced high-quality goods. At the core, we aim to add value to the employment ecosystem by promoting women employment and providing opportunities in off-farm activities in the rural communities of Dhankuta. For our partner farmers, we strive to provide a reliable pricing mechanism and introduce new farming technologies and practices to their areas.
